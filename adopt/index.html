ADOPTABLE KITTIES

Thank you for choosing to open your heart to a cat or kitten in need of a home!

All of our adoptable cats and kittens live in foster homes until adoption.
We do not have a brick and mortar building â€” but we promise to make the process simple, smooth and memorable.

If you find a cat listed below that you would like to meet/adopt, please fill out an application. Application review takes 2-5 business days. Learn more about our adoption process here.

Adoption Process

  • Fill out Application
  • 2-5 days for Application Review
  • Meet & Greet with Kitty
  • Make adoption payment online via credit/debit
    (We no longer accept cash/check)
  • Finalize adoption day with foster family

Adoption Fees

  • Kittens (Under 7 months) $165
  • Two Kittens $305
    Why kittens should be adopted in pairs!
  • Adult Cats (7 months and over) $85

Adoption Fees Include:

  • Spay/Neuter
  • Rabies vaccine (Age appropriate)
  • Distemper vaccine(s) (Age appropriate)
  • Microchip
