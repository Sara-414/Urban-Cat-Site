Adoptable Kitties

Thank you for choosing to open your heart to a cat or kittens in need of a home! Our adoptable felines are socialized and each has their own story — many found living in colonies by our Trap-Neuter-Return team.

All of our adoptable cats and kittens live in foster homes until adoption.

We do not have a brick and mortar building — but we promise to make the process simple, smooth and memorable. We want you and your new kitty to be happy together!

If you find a cat listed below that you would like to add to your family, please fill out an application. Application review takes 2-5 business days. Learn more about our adoption process here.
