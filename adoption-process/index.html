ADOPTION PROCESS

We are a best match rescue. Our goal is to match the cat(s) to a home where they would be happiest and most successful. It is not first-come, first-served.

After finding a cat or kittens you would like to meet (or if you prefer to adopt a cat recommended by our adoption experts) an application is filled out. The Urban Cat Coalition adoption team will then perform background checks, veterinarian reference checks and landlord checks. Once the application review process is complete, you will be contacted by one of our adoption team members who will e-mail both you and the foster to arrange a meet and greet (virtual or in-person) with the kitty you want to adopt.

Once you have met the kitty, you and the foster home will decide if it is a good fit. We have a 24 hour hold after the meeting to allow you time to be sure adopting is the right decision. When you have made your decision to adopt, you will receive a link from our adoption team to our online database where you can sign off on the contract and pay your adoption fee from the comfort of your own computer or mobile phone. Once you sign that contract and pay the adoption fee, the foster will arrange a day and time for you to get your new furry companion. We will email you digital copies of all medical records and register the microchip to you as well as give you access to an online database called AdopterLuv.

Applicants must be at least 21 years of age to be eligible for adopting from Urban Cat Coalition.

Adoption Fees

Please note - adoption fees must be paid by credit or debit card. We can no longer accept cash or check.

  • Kittens (Under 7 months) $165
  • Two Kittens $305
  • Adult Cats (7 months and over) $85

Adoption Fees Include:

  • Spay/Neuter
  • Rabies vaccine (Age appropriate)
  • FVRCP shot(s) (Age appropriate)
  • Preventative Dewormer
  • Microchip with lifetime membership
