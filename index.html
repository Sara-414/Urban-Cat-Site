Urban Cat Coalition is dedicated to helping create a no-kill community by humanely reducing the community cat population in the Milwaukee area through targeted trap-neuter-return (TNR)

It is estimated that there are about 70 million stray cats in the United States alone.

Every year approximately 1.4 million cats are killed in US shelters.

Less than 5% of stray cats are reclaimed from shelters by their owners.

WHAT IS TNR?

Trap-Neuter-Return (TNR) is the only proven method of humanely reducing the cat population. Community cats are trapped, then taken to a veterinarian to be fixed, vaccinated, and ear tipped. The cats are held in a safe space for recovery before being released back to their neighborhood. They are provided food and shelter by community cat caretakers.

Sterilizing prevents more cats from being born on the streets. TNR reduces the number of cats entering shelters, and reduces shelter deaths!
