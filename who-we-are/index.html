ABOUT URBAN CAT COALITION

We are a volunteer-run rescue organization that conducts targeted humane trapping of colony cats and offers support for community cat caretakers.

WHO WE HELP

Our focus is on assisting cats most overlooked or unaided by other rescue organizations:

  • Feral cats
  • Neonates
  • Spicy kittens
  • Injured street cats
  • Shy cats and kittens deemed unadoptable at MADACC

WHO WE HAVE TO TURN AWAY

  • Owner surrenders
  • Friendly cats trapped amongst colony cats
  • Microchipped cats assumed to be dumped (though we will try to reunite with owner)
  • Socialized kittens
  • Friendly cats abandoned in homes
  • Community members needing financial assistance neutering their personal cats

WHY CAN'T WE HELP THEM ALL?

We specialize in rescuing outdoor community cats that are unowned and injured or deemed unadoptable. Our focus on these cats is crucial, as no other organization is currently addressing their needs.

GUIDANCE FOR THOSE WE'RE UNABLE TO ASSIST

There are several rescues in Milwaukee that will help FRIENDLY CATS. Please refer any (non-UCC) surrenders to those programs: Almost Home MKE, Happy Endings, Second Hand Purrs, and Wisconsin Humane Society.

Additionally, there are initiatives in place to support individuals with spay/neuter vouchers, facilitated by Friends of MADACC and WHS Spay/Neuter Clinic.
